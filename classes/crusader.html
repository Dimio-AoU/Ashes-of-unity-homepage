Crusader

Frontline holy DPS wielding light-infused weapons and fearless charges.

Role & Fantasy

Radiant executor who smites foes with consecrated steel. Excels at burst windows, execution thresholds, and punishing the condemned.

Signature Tools

  • Judgment Strike: Bonus damage vs. marked targets.
  • Consecrated Charge: Gap closer that empowers the next attack.
  • Radiant Execution: Execute at low HP with holy burst.

Team Value

Brings reliable front-line pressure and anti-corruption bonuses vs. shadow / undead.
