Arcane Order

Knowledge, power, and controlled chaos.

Lore

The Arcane Order rejects divinity, worshiping knowledge itself. The Core is a cosmic engine, not a god.

Culture

Floating citadels, rune towers, and laboratories ablaze with glyph-light and unstable energy.

Goals

Unlock the Core’s true purpose; transcend mortal limits.

Rivals & Allies

Rivals: Knights Order; Holy Empire.

Allies: Shadowborn.

Player Fantasy

You are the mage who defies gods—bending the world to your design.

Classes

  • Spellblade — Physical (Short Range) — STR, INT
  • Elementalist — Hybrid (Mid Range) — INT, Affinity
  • Arcanist — Magic (Long Range) — INT, WIS
  • Arcstrider — Physical (Long Range) — DEX, INT
