Templar

Hybrid • Mid Range — Stats: VIT, WIS

Shield-bearer of faith. Projects radiant wards, punishes darkness, and anchors the frontline.

Signature Abilities
  • Ward of Aegis — Party barrier with damage conversion.
  • Bulwark Bash — Stagger + taunt cone.
  • Sanctuary Line — Ground aura; allies gain mitigation & regen.
  • Reflect of Faith — Brief spell reflect against hexes.
Progression
  • Awakening: Seraphic Bastion (mobile warding dome).
  • Succession: Iron Parapet (heavy tank/control focus).
  • Prestige: Radiant Ascension deepens ward synergies.
Role

Tank-support hybrid that stabilizes pushes and counters curses.

Faction Synergy

Pairs with Crusader for dive plays; empowers Cleric heals and Sunstrider volleys.
