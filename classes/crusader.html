Crusader

Physical • Short Range — Stats: STR, VIT

Holy Empire’s front-line zealot. A radiant greatsword knight who carves through corruption with consecrated steel and stuns foes with judgmental smites.

Signature Abilities
  • Smite Cleave — Wide holy slash; builds Radiance.
  • Vow of Purity — Self-buff: +block, minor cleanse on hit.
  • Sanctified Rush — Gap closer; next strike stuns.
  • Judgment Breaker — Heavy finisher; bonus vs. corrupted.
Progression
  • Awakening: Radiant Vanguard (bulwark + cleave auras).
  • Succession: Inquisitorial Blade (single-target executioner).
  • Prestige: Radiant Ascension path synergy.
Role

Durable melee DPS with stuns, cleaves, and anti-corruption bonuses.

Faction Synergy

Amplified by Cleric auras and Templar wards; pairs well with Sunstrider marks.
