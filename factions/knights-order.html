Knights Order

Discipline, strategy, and magitek supremacy.

Lore

Formed after the Core’s fall, the Knights guard civilization with iron discipline and pragmatic arcane engineering.

Culture

Strongholds and sapphire banners. Duty, honor, sacrifice. Forges and academies build siege engines and rune arms.

Goals

Unite the fractured world under one banner; regulate Unity Shards; hold the line against chaos.

Rivals & Allies

Rivals: Arcane Order; Shadowborn.

Allies: Holy Empire.

Player Fantasy

You are the warlord or spellblade general—order through steel and strategy.

Classes

  • Steel Knight — Physical (Short Range) — STR, END
  • Battle Mage — Hybrid (Mid Range) — STR, INT
  • Arcane Sentinel — Magic (Long Range) — INT, END
  • Arbalist — Physical (Long Range) — DEX, END
