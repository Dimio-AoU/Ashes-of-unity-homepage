Arbalist

Physical • Long Range — Stats: DEX, END

Magitek crossbowman deploying traps and explosive volleys to dominate lanes and walls.

Signature Abilities
  • Detonator Bolt — Explosive shot; chain on marked targets.
  • Rune Snare — Trap that roots & reveals.
  • Volley Protocol — Burst mode; drains stamina.
  • Ballista Call — Deploy a short-lived siege turret.
Progression
  • Awakening: Siege Warden (stronger deployables).
  • Succession: Deadeye (crit & precision long shots).
  • Prestige: Knights of the Glory enhances banner buffs.
Role

Long-range physical DPS with trap control and siege synergy.

Faction Synergy

Thrives under Sentinel wards; benefits from Steel Knight phalanx holds.
