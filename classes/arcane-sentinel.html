Arcane Sentinel

Anti-mage bulwark—counters, wards, and spell denial fields.

Role & Fantasy

Specialist defender who dismantles hostile spellcasters and stabilizes lanes with suppression zones.

Signature Tools

  • Null Ward: Area that reduces magic damage and muffles casts.
  • Counterseal: Parry a spell to reflect or ground it.
  • Runic Bastion: Team magic resistance spike.
