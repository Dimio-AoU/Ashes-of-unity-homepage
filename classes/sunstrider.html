Sunstrider

Physical • Long Range — Stats: DEX, WIS

Radiant archer. Blessed bolts pierce corruption and explode into sunbursts from afar.

Signature Abilities
  • Solar Mark — Tag targets; allies deal bonus radiant damage.
  • Arrow of Dawn — Piercing shot with holy detonation.
  • Gleamstep — Short blink, leaves a flare that reveals stealth.
  • Sanctified Volley — Area rain of radiant arrows.
Progression
  • Awakening: Parhelion Ranger (multi-mark & chain bursts).
  • Succession: Wardensight (precision crit build + reveals).
  • Prestige: Radiant Ascension expands volley control.
Role

Long-range physical DPS with team marks and anti-stealth tools.

Faction Synergy

Marks empower Crusader/Templar finishers; Cleric extends survivability.
