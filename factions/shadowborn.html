Shadowborn

Corruption, deception, and survival in the dark.

Lore

Exiled and hunted, the Shadowborn turned to poison, curse, and shadowcraft. To them, fragments are cursed gifts of freedom.

Culture

Hidden camps, masks, and whispered bargains. Shadowflame torches, bone charms, and poison cauldrons.

Goals

Topple empires, spread fear, and fuel forbidden crafts with shards.

Rivals & Allies

Rivals: Holy Empire; Knights Order.

Allies: Arcane Order.

Player Fantasy

You are the blade in the dark—the cursed survivor who fights dirty and thrives in chaos.

Classes

  • Assassin — Physical (Short Range) — DEX, AGI
  • Hexblade — Hybrid (Mid Range) — STR, INT
  • Necromancer — Magic (Long Range) — INT, Corruption
  • Wraithbow — Physical (Long Range) — DEX, Corruption
