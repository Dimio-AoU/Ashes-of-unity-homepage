Dark Knight

Bruiser tank—drains life and hardens armor with bloodcraft.

Role & Fantasy

Relentless frontline presence trading health for power, then stealing it back.

Signature Tools

  • Blood Ward: Convert damage dealt into temporary armor.
  • Dread Cleave: Wide swing that heals per target hit.
  • Grim Challenge: Lock a foe into a duel zone briefly.
