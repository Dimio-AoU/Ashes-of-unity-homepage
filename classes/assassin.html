Assassin

Physical • Short Range — Stats: DEX, AGI

Stealth duelist specializing in ambushes, poisons, and surgical eliminations.

Signature Abilities
  • Veilstep — Enter stealth; next attack deals bonus crit.
  • Venom Kiss — Applies stacking poison and weakens healing.
  • Shadow Reel — Short yank or blink to target’s back.
  • Quietus — Execute from stealth; massive burst.
Progression
  • Awakening: Night Reaper (chain stealth & mobility).
  • Succession: Venomblade Focus (DoT and sustain).
  • Prestige: Blood Oath Pact unlocks shadow boons.
Role

High-burst pick potential; thrives in chaos and flanks.

Faction Synergy

Hexblade curses amplify your kills; Necromancer zones cover the retreat.
