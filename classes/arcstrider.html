Arcstrider

Physical • Long Range — Stats: DEX, INT

Energy marksman who condenses raw mana into charged shots and explosive runes.

Signature Abilities
  • Charge Shot — Hold to overcharge; pierces formations.
  • Rune Mine — Deployable explosive glyph with slow.
  • Focus Window — Short buff; next shot crits and detonates.
  • Mana Barrage — Rapid arc shots; drains focus.
Progression
  • Awakening: Aether Sniper (longer charge & super pierce).
  • Succession: Runegunner (mine synergies & mobility).
  • Prestige: Codex of Eternity doubles focus windows.
Role

Long-range burst DPS that opens picks and lane breaks.

Faction Synergy

Arcanist runes shape fights; Elementalist sets up shatter windows.
