Spellblade

Physical • Short Range — Stats: STR, INT

Arcane duelist channeling mana through blades for explosive counters and finishers.

Signature Abilities
  • Manablade — Basic hits store arcane charges.
  • Arc Counter — Parry → releases a crescent wave.
  • Flux Step — Short dash; next hit shocks in a line.
  • Overcast — Spend all charges for massive finisher.
Progression

  • Succession: Aether Fencer (sustained weave play).
  • Prestige: Codex of Eternity doubles charge tricks.
Role

Short-range DPS with high-skill parries and charge management.

Faction Synergy

Elementalist zones feed safe engages; Arcanist runes boost consistency.
