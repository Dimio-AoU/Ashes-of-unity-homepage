Arcanist

Magic • Long Range — Stats: INT, WIS

Runic geometry and sigil fields amplify allies, trap foes, and shape the fight from afar.

Signature Abilities
  • Sigil of Power — Boost allied spell potency in area.
  • Binding Seal — Root/suppress zone with delay.
  • Ward Script — Quick barrier; stacks with Sentinel walls.
  • Runeburst — Remote detonation of placed sigils.
Progression
  • Awakening: Grand Scribe (multi-sigil orchestration).
  • Succession: Spell Architect (support/control specialist).
  • Prestige: Codex of Eternity empowers chain scripts.
Role

Long-range caster support/control that elevates allied bursts.

Faction Synergy

Pairs with Elementalist rotations and Arcstrider kill-windows.
