Elementalist

Hybrid • Mid Range — Stats: INT, Affinity

Chains fire, frost, and lightning; hybrid AoE DPS that shapes chaos into elegant control.

Signature Abilities
  • Triad Weave — Rotate elements; next cast is empowered.
  • Frost Lattice — Slow field; amplifies shatter damage.
  • Storm Lash — Chain lightning that favors marked foes.
  • Inferno Bloom — Delayed AoE; detonates on crowd-control.
Progression
  • Awakening: Cataclysmic Savant (bigger chained combos).
  • Succession: Resonant Adept (safer, sustained rotation).
  • Prestige: Codex of Eternity grants double-empower procs.
Role

Mid-range hybrid AoE control/DPS with elemental synergies.

Faction Synergy

Arcanist runes supercharge rotations; Arcstrider bursts clean up.
