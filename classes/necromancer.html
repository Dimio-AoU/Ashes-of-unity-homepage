Necromancer

Magic • Long Range — Stats: INT, Corruption

Death’s scholar; commands rot, summons bone minions, and siphons life from afar.

Signature Abilities
  • Blight Orb — Spreading rot field; slows and drains.
  • Raise Bones — Temporary skeletal guardians.
  • Soul Leech — Beam that heals while channeling.
  • Grave Quiet — Silence zone; strong vs. casters.
Progression
  • Awakening: Lichborne (persistent minions & aura rot).
  • Succession: Plaguecaller (DoT amplification & control).
  • Prestige: Blood Oath Pact augments siphon traits.
Role

Long-range controller with sustain and area denial.

Faction Synergy

Assassin capitalizes on your silences; Hexblade detonates your rot stacks.
