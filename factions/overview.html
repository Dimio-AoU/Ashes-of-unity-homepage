What This Page Covers

The Factions Overview defines the four core powers of Ashes of Unity: Holy Empire, Knights Order, Shadowborn, and Arcane Order. Each faction includes iconic classes, unique attributes, and a legendary hidden questline that reinforces its identity.

Faction Philosophy

Good Factions (Holy Empire, Knights Order): emphasize defense, healing, formation control, and order ‚Äî slower to start, but strong sustain and counterplay.

Evil Factions (Shadowborn, Arcane Order): emphasize offense, corruption, burst, and chaos ‚Äî fast, powerful, but fragile if misplayed.

Prestige paths can hybridize identities, but the core fantasy remains intact for each faction.

World Role

Factions fight not just for land, but for the right to define the meaning of Unity Shards ‚Äî divine relic, weapon of order, cursed poison, or cosmic engine. Every conflict, raid, and war is ultimately a battle for the Core‚Äôs legacy.
