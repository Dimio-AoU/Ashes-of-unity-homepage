Steel Knight

Vanguard tank—fortified lines, defensive auras, and formation control.

Role & Fantasy

A bulwark in magitek plate who anchors the front. Soaks pressure and locks down choke points for the Order’s advance.

Signature Tools

  • Bulwark Stance: Heavy mitigation with reduced mobility.
  • Phalanx Wall: Projected barrier that allies can fight behind.
  • Taunt Protocol: Compels enemies to focus the Knight briefly.
