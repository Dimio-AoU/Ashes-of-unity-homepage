Hexblade

Hybrid • Mid Range — Stats: STR, INT

Curse-infused fighter weaving dark runes into weapon arts and bleed-heavy combos.

Signature Abilities
  • Rune Carve — Basic hits apply stacking hex.
  • Curse Bloom — Detonate stacks for AoE bleed.
  • Shadow Guard — Brief parry; counters apply Weakness.
  • Oath Sever — Heavy strike scaling with stacks.
Progression
  • Awakening: Doomcarver (bigger DoTs & chain detonation).
  • Succession: Blackguard (parry/counter specialist).
  • Prestige: Blood Oath Pact enhances corruption scaling.
Role

Mid-range hybrid DPS with curse management and bleed explosions.

Faction Synergy

Pairs with Assassin burst and Necromancer rot for lethal overlaps.
