Holy Empire

Zeal, Order, and Radiant Might guided by divine faith.

Classes

Crusader (Physical Attack)

Frontline holy DPS wielding light-infused weapons. Bursts of radiant damage, executions against the unworthy, and fearless charges.

Templar (Tank / Support Hybrid)

Radiant bulwark. Projects barriers and auras that fortify allies, punishes foes who strike the faithful, and anchors the line.

Cleric (Magic Defense / Healer)

Miracles and mercy. Purifies curses, restores vitality, and shields the party with sanctified wards and blessings.

Inquisitor (Hybrid DPS / Judge)

Placeholder derived from Holy Empire lore. Relentless judge of heresy: brands enemies with verdicts, amplifies damage against condemned targets, and uses radiant crowd control to isolate sinners.

Legendary Quest

Radiant Ascension

Prestige path unlocking Blessing of Eternity and advanced evolutions that embody the Empire’s divine mandate.
