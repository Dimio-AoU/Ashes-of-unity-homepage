How Balance Works

Light vs Shadow • Order vs Chaos • The World Hangs in Equilibrium

Faction Balance

Holy Empire counters Shadowborn corruption and withstands Arcane chaos through faith and resilience.

Knights Order shields against Arcane onslaughts and uses discipline to outmaneuver Shadowborn ambushes.

Shadowborn break holy lines with poisons, stealth, and speed — but struggle against Knightly defenses.

Arcane Order overwhelms with elemental destruction and mana control, yet falters against Knightly precision.

Equilibrium

Holy ⚔ Shadowborn • Knights ⚔ Arcane • All stand equal beneath the World Core.

No faction dominates — victory belongs to strategy, terrain, and player mastery.
