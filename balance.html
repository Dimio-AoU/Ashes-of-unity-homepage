Balance of the Four

Light vs Shadow • Order vs Chaos — equilibrium forged by mastery, timing, and terrain.

Each faction excels in its identity and faces natural counters — not hard rock-paper-scissors, but strategic edges that reward skill.

Holy Empire

Radiance

Resilient lines, radiant shields, disciplined pushes. Cleanses corruption and stabilizes fights with superior sustain.

Edge: counters Shadowborn attrition and outlasts burst with mitigation and healing.

VS

Shadowborn

Corruption

Ambush, poison, and cursecraft. Break formations, drain resources, and punish mistakes with lethal precision.

Edge: bypasses rigid lines and punishes slow responses with bursts and DoTs.

Knights Order

Discipline

Formations, anti-magic bulwarks, and battlefield control. Wins through positioning, leadership, and attrition.

Edge: blunts Arcane volatility and forces clean, punishable trades.

VS

Arcane Order

Anomaly

Elemental devastation, AoE tempos, and mana warfare. Breaks lines with overwhelming power and control.

Edge: overwhelms static play and punishes clustered formations.

Design Principle

There is no absolute winner. Each matchup has tools and answers — mastery, comps, and terrain create the win condition.

Result: Holy ⚔ Shadowborn • Knights ⚔ Arcane • Cross-counters remain situational and skill-based.
