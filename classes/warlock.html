Warlock

Hexes and drains—attrition through stacking curses and siphoned strength.

Role & Fantasy

Bleeds enemies over time and twists fate with debilitating maledictions.

Signature Tools

  • Wither: DoT that ramps the longer it remains.
  • Life Tap: Drain target to heal yourself briefly.
  • Hex Matrix: Converts active curses into a burst.
