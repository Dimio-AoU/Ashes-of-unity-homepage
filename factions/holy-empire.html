Holy Empire

Radiance, order, and divine zeal.

Lore

The Holy Empire believes the World Core’s fragments are divine relics scattered by their god of Light. They are zealous stewards destined to purify corruption and bring eternal order.

Culture

Golden spires, ever-burning braziers, and sacred law. Outsiders call them oppressive zealots; within, devotion is sacred duty.

Goals

Reclaim every Unity Shard, sanctify corrupted lands, convert or destroy unbelievers.

Rivals & Allies

Rivals: Shadowborn; Arcane Order.

Allies: Knights Order.

Player Fantasy

You are the radiant crusader—the last bastion of light.

Classes

  • Crusader — Physical (Short Range) — STR, VIT
  • Templar — Hybrid (Mid Range) — VIT, WIS
  • Cleric — Magic (Long Range) — WIS, INT
  • Sunstrider — Physical (Long Range) — DEX, WIS
