Game Systems

Leveling • Stats • Skills • Prestige

Leveling

Players begin at level 1 and can reach level 200. Progression unlocks stat points, skill points, and prestige paths.

Stats & Stat Points

Core: STR, DEX, INT, VIT, END. Hidden stats emerge through quests and rare events: Luck, Perception, Radiance, Corruption, Affinity, Leadership, etc.

Skills & Skill Points

Skill lines (Class, Weapon, Armor, Faction, Universal) level independently. Skills can morph, awaken, or succeed into stronger variants.

Prestige

At cap thresholds, characters re-emerge into prestige paths, retain growth, and unlock legendary questlines unique to each faction.
