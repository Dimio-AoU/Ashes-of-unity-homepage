Arcane Sentinel

Magic • Long Range — Stats: INT, END

Anti-mage defender who deploys bulwarks, reflects, and suppression wards.

Signature Abilities
  • Null Field — Zone reduces enemy spell potency.
  • Spellward — Directional barrier; blocks projectiles.
  • Reflective Veil — Short window of reflects & dampening.
  • Counterglyph — Tag a caster; next spell fizzles.
Progression
  • Awakening: Bastion Arcanum (mobile ward platforms).
  • Succession: Static Aegis (fortified bunker build).
  • Prestige: Knights of the Glory strengthens counters.
Role

Protective caster that shuts down enemy mages and protects formations.

Faction Synergy

Pairs with Arbalist trap zones and Steel Knight phalanx for siege holds.
