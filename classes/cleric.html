Cleric

Magic • Long Range — Stats: WIS, INT

Primary healer and purifier. Radiant pulses restore allies and scour shadow from the field.

Signature Abilities
  • Hymn of Renewal — Cone heal with regen.
  • Lightwell — Place a spring that pulses healing + cleanse.
  • Sunlash — Radiant beam; deals bonus to undead/hexed.
  • Aegis Chant — Short invulnerability on an ally.
Progression
  • Awakening: Seraphic Choir (mobile healing procession).
  • Succession: Dawnkeeper (burst heal + barrier play).
  • Prestige: Radiant Ascension unlocks Blessing of Eternity.
Role

Long-range healer/support with cleanse and radiant control tools.

Faction Synergy

Supercharges Crusader/Templar uptime; primes Sunstrider crit windows.
