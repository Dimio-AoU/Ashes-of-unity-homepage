Templar

Radiant bulwark that fortifies allies with barriers and auras.

Role & Fantasy

Anchor the line with sanctified shields and punishing counters against those who strike your allies.

Signature Tools

  • Ward of Light: Team barrier that scales with faith.
  • Retribution Guard: Reflect a portion of damage briefly.
  • Sanctum: Zone that reduces incoming damage and cleanses.
